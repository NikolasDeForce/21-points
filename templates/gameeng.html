RULES

1. The dealer deals two cards to each player.

2. All cards give the player a certain number of points: ace — 1 point or 11 points, king — 4 points, queen — 3 points, jack — 2 points, the rest of the cards according to their face value.

3. The player's goal is to collect 21 points or the number closest to it.

4. After the player has received two cards, he can ask for more.

5. When the dealer has given the players as many cards as they asked for, the players open up and show the cards.
